## Full Description (4000 chars max — used for both stores)

CareEquity connects patients with minority physicians and culturally competent healthcare providers in their area. Whether you need a doctor who shares your cultural background, speaks your language, or understands your community's health concerns, CareEquity makes it easy to find and book an appointment with a provider you can trust.

### Why CareEquity?

Health outcomes improve when patients see providers who understand their cultural context. CareEquity was built to close the gap between minority patients and minority physicians — making culturally affirming care discoverable and accessible for everyone.

### Key Features

- Search by specialty, insurance plan, and distance
- Filter by physician identity tags (cultural background, languages spoken)
- View verified provider profiles with bio, availability, and patient reviews
- Book appointments directly in the app
- Save your care team for quick future access
- Manage appointments and cancel with one tap
- Works on both iPhone and Android

### Verified Providers

Every physician listed on CareEquity goes through a multi-tier verification process:

- Tier 1 — Professional: NPI license validation
- Tier 2 — Identity: Government ID verification
- Tier 3 — Practice: Physical practice address confirmation

Verified badges appear on provider cards so you always know who has been confirmed.

### Privacy First

CareEquity does not sell your personal data. Location access is used only to surface nearby providers and is never stored on our servers without your explicit consent. All health-related information you share stays within the CareEquity platform.

### Supported Insurance

We index providers that accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, Humana, UnitedHealth, Medicaid, Medicare, and many more. Use the insurance filter to show only in-network providers.
